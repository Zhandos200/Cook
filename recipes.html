Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Black Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk the eggs and cheese together in a bowl.
  4. Mix everything together with the pasta, adding pepper to taste.

Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Fresh Mozzarella Cheese
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough and spread the tomato sauce evenly.
  3. Top with slices of fresh mozzarella and basil leaves.
  4. Drizzle olive oil and sprinkle with salt.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:

  • Basmati Rice
  • Chicken (cut into pieces)
  • Onions (sliced)
  • Yogurt
  • Biryani Masala
  • Saffron
  • Mint Leaves

Instructions:

  1. Soak basmati rice for 30 minutes, then boil until 70% cooked.
  2. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, biryani masala, and salt for 1 hour.
  3. In a pot, fry onions until golden, then add the marinated chicken and cook until tender.
  4. Layer the chicken and rice, sprinkle with saffron water and mint leaves.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes.

French Croissants

Ingredients:

  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Butter (cold)
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Egg (for egg wash)

Instructions:

  1. Make dough by combining flour, yeast, sugar, and milk, then refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  2. Roll out cold butter and layer it into the dough, folding and rolling it multiple times.
  3. Cut into triangles, roll into croissant shapes, and let rise for 1 hour.
  4. Brush with egg wash and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 15-20 minutes.

Pad Thai

Ingredients:

  • Rice Noodles
  • Tofu or Shrimp
  • Bean Sprouts
  • Peanuts (crushed)
  • Tamarind Paste
  • Fish Sauce
  • Eggs
  • Lime

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice noodles in warm water for 30 minutes.
  2. Stir-fry tofu or shrimp in a hot pan.
  3. Push tofu to the side, crack in eggs, and scramble.
  4. Add noodles, tamarind paste, fish sauce, and bean sprouts. Stir-fry until well mixed.
  5. Top with peanuts and serve with lime wedges.

Banana Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 2 Bananas (mashed)
  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • Butter (for cooking)
  • Maple Syrup (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mash the bananas and mix in the egg, milk, and vanilla extract.
  2. Add the flour and baking powder, stirring until the batter is smooth.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and melt a little butter.
  4. Pour the batter into the pan to form pancakes, cooking for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  5. Serve with maple syrup and additional banana slices, if desired.

Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 2 cups Ricotta Cheese
  • 2 cups Mozzarella Cheese (shredded)
  • 2 cups Marinara Sauce
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese (grated)
  • 1 Egg
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and cook the ground beef until browned. Add marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  5. In a baking dish, layer lasagna noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and shredded mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  6. Top with parmesan cheese and bake for 25-30 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  7. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.

Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Cooked Chicken (diced)
  • 1 cup Mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup Celery (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup Red Onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup Fresh Parsley (chopped)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chicken, celery, red onion, and parsley.
  2. In a small bowl, mix the mayonnaise, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Combine the dressing with the chicken mixture and stir well.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to let the flavors meld.

Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Broccoli florets
  • 1 cup Bell Peppers (sliced)
  • 1 cup Carrots (sliced)
  • 1 cup Snow Peas
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Ginger (minced)
  • 2 Garlic Cloves (minced)
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, and sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Add the broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, and snow peas. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes or until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Add soy sauce and stir well to coat vegetables.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.

Tiramisu

Ingredients:

  • 6 Egg Yolks
  • 1 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1 cup Mascarpone Cheese
  • 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • 1 cup Espresso (cooled)
  • 24 Ladyfingers
  • Cocoa Powder (for dusting)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk egg yolks and sugar until pale and thick.
  2. Fold in mascarpone cheese until smooth.
  3. In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form. Fold into mascarpone mixture.
  4. Dip ladyfingers into espresso briefly and layer them in a serving dish.
  5. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat layers.
  6. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.
  7. Before serving, dust with cocoa powder.
